Our Story

Family-Owned. Zacatecas-Proud.

Birria Patro Jaramillo brings the authentic flavors of Zacatecas, Mexico straight to San Jose. Family-owned and operated since 2015, we slow-cook our birria with traditional recipes passed down through generations — lamb and beef, simmered low and slow in a rich chili broth, served with house-made tortillas and steaming consomé.

Our signature "Birria Revolcada" — grilled birria with charred onions — has earned us a devoted following across the Bay Area. Every bite is the real thing. We also offer catering for your next event.
