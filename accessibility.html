Accessibility

Our commitment to an accessible experience for every guest.

Our Commitment

Birria Patro Jaramillo is committed to making our website usable by everyone, including guests who use assistive technology such as screen readers, screen magnifiers, and keyboard-only navigation. We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA success criteria across this site.

Built-In Accessibility Features

  • Skip to main content link at the top of every page, visible on keyboard focus.
  • Keyboard navigation for all interactive elements — menus, the cart, modals, the photo gallery, and the accessibility widget.
  • Color contrast — all text on this site is designed to meet a 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio.
  • Descriptive alt text on every meaningful image, including all food and restaurant photos.
  • Semantic HTML and ARIA labels on buttons, dialogs, and form fields to support screen readers.

The Accessibility Widget

Click the ♿ button in the bottom-left corner of any page to open our accessibility panel. From there you can adjust:

  • Text Size — scale text from 90% up to 130%.
  • Contrast — switch between Normal, High Contrast (black background, white text, yellow links), or Dark Mode.
  • Font — switch to a dyslexia-friendly typeface.
  • Highlight Links — add a visible underline to every link on the page.
  • Pause Animations — stop scrolling animations, carousels, and transitions for guests sensitive to motion.
  • Reading Guide — a horizontal line that follows your cursor to help track text while reading.

All preferences are saved on your device and applied automatically the next time you visit. Use Reset All in the panel at any time to return to the default experience.
