The Birria Story

Zacatecas Heritage. San Jose Home.

Birria Patro Jaramillo brings the authentic flavors of Zacatecas, Mexico straight to San Jose. Family-owned and operated since 2015, we slow-cook our birria with traditional recipes passed down through generations — simmered low and slow in a rich chili broth, served with house-made tortillas and steaming consomé.

"We make it the way it's always been made — slow, careful, and with everything we've got."
